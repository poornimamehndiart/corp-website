About Us

Crafting Stories Through Majestic Mehndi Art

Welcome to Poornima Mehndi Art

Our journey began with a passion for bringing beauty and meaning to every occasion through beautiful mehndi patterns because we believe that "Every Design Tells A Story".

At Poornima Mehndi Art, we specialize in a wide range of styles, from timeless bridal henna to contemporary and festive designs. Every stroke is a testament to our commitment to excellence, precision, and creativity. Whether it’s a wedding, festival, or personal celebration, we aim to make your moments memorable with our artistic touch.

With years of experience and countless satisfied clients, we take pride in transforming your hands and feet into canvases of stunning art. Let us be part of your story and create designs that tell your story with elegance and charm!
